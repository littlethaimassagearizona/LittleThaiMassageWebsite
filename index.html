About

A small studio with experienced hands.

Little Thai Massage is a family-run studio in North Scottsdale. Our therapists are trained in nuad boran — the traditional Thai practice that pairs rhythmic acupressure along the body's energy lines with assisted stretching.

We keep the room quiet, the pace unhurried, and the number of clients on the schedule small. If you've been to a chain spa and left feeling rushed, this is the opposite of that.

First Time?

A few quick answers.

  • What should I wear?
    For traditional Thai massage, wear loose, comfortable clothing — the session is performed fully clothed on a floor mat. For Swedish, deep tissue, or facial, undress to your comfort under the sheet on the table.
  • Do I need to bring anything?
    No. Arrive five to ten minutes early so we can settle you in. Drink water before and after.
  • Is parking available?
    Yes — free parking in front of the studio at 7124 E Becker Lane, Unit A.
  • Do you accept tips?
    Cash tips are appreciated and never expected.
  • How do I pay?
    Pay securely by card when you book online, or pay by cash or card at the studio.
  • What is your cancellation policy?
    Free cancellation at least 2 hours before your appointment. Cancellations within 2 hours are charged 50% of the service. No-shows are charged in full.
